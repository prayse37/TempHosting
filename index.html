What is MOXA

The Ancient Art of
Moxibustion

Moxibustion (뜸, moxa therapy) is a Traditional Korean & Chinese Medicine technique with over 2,000 years of history.

🌿

Dried Mugwort · 쑥

Moxa is made from Artemisia argyi — dried and aged mugwort — which is burned slowly near or on acupuncture points to deliver therapeutic warmth deep into the body's meridian channels.

2,000+ Years of
Traditional Use

In Traditional Korean Medicine, the body's vital energy — 기 (Qi/Ki) — flows through invisible pathways called meridians. When these pathways become blocked or imbalanced, illness and pain follow.

Moxibustion works by applying gentle, penetrating heat at specific acupuncture points. This warmth stimulates the flow of Qi and blood, dissolves stagnation, and restores the body's natural healing capacity.

Unlike acupuncture, moxa does not require needles. Its gentle heat is accessible, safe for home use, and especially effective for cold, deficient, or chronic conditions.

How it Works

Three Steps to
Healing

Moxibustion activates the body's own healing system through a simple, time-tested process.

01

Locate the Point · 혈자리 찾기

Specific acupuncture points — such as Jok-sam-ri (足三里) or Gi-hae (氣海) — are identified on the body. Each point connects to meridian pathways that influence different organs and functions.

02

Apply the Heat · 열 자극

A small cone or stick of dried mugwort is lit and held near the skin (indirect moxa) or placed directly on the point (direct moxa). The warmth penetrates deep into tissue, stimulating circulation and Qi flow.

03

Restore Balance · 균형 회복

Regular sessions dissolve blockages, warm cold areas, strengthen deficient energy, and support the body's natural immune and healing responses — leading to lasting improvement in health and vitality.

Why MOXA

Conditions MOXA
Helps

Clinical tradition and modern research support moxibustion's effectiveness across a wide range of conditions.

🦴

Pain Management

Knee pain, lower back pain, shoulder and wrist pain, arthritis, plantar fasciitis, and joint stiffness respond well to targeted moxa protocols.

🛡️

Immune Boost

Regular moxa on key immune points strengthens the body's defensive energy (衛氣), reducing frequency of colds and chronic illness.

🌡️

Cold & Deficiency

Ideal for cold extremities, fatigue, low vitality, and yang deficiency — moxa warms the deepest channels where cold settles.

🥢

Digestive Health

Points like Zhong-wan (中脘) and Zu-san-li (足三里) strengthen digestive function, relieve bloating, and improve nutrient absorption.

🧓

Senior Care

Gentle and needle-free — moxa is ideal for elderly care. It builds leg strength, prevents falls, and supports healthy aging and mobility.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Self-Care

Once learned, moxa can be safely practiced at home for the whole family — making it a powerful tool for independent health management.
